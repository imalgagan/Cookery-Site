Chicken StirFry

A delicious and easy-to-make appetizer inspired by a local pizzeria.

Ingredients:

  • Skinless chicken breasts
  • Broccoli florets
  • Bell pepper
  • Carrot
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add chicken slices and cook until browned. Remove from the wok.
  4. In the same wok, add more oil if needed. Stir-fry broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot until crisp-tender.
  5. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the wok and pour the sauce over. Stir to combine.
  7. Serve the chicken stir-fry over cooked rice.
