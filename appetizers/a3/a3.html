Caprese Salad

A delicious and easy-to-make appetizer inspired by a local pizzeria.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange alternating slices of tomatoes and mozzarella on a serving platter.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
