Vegetarian Quinoa Bowl

A delicious and easy-to-make appetizer inspired by a local pizzeria.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine quinoa and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer until quinoa is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  3. Toss the ingredients together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Divide the quinoa mixture into bowls. Top with avocado slices, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
