No-Bake Cheesecake

A delightful dessert with mascarpone, Pedro Xim√©nez sherry, and pandoro!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Cherry or strawberry pie filling (for topping)

Method

  1. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Pour the cream cheese filling over the crust in the springform pan
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  6. Before serving, spread a layer of cherry or strawberry pie filling over the top.
